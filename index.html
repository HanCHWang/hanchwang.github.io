About me

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in Control Group , University of Oxford. I am very lucky to be supervised by Prof. Antonis Papachristodoulou and Prof. Kostas Margellos. Before joining Oxford, I got my B.S. in information security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and worked as a research assistant at FINS Lab.

My research interest mainly lies in control theory and optimization, with applications to robotics and smart cities. I am especially interested in Safety, which is a basic property of dynamical systems like Stability.
