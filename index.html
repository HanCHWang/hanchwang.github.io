About me

I'm a thir-year Ph.D. student in Control Group , University of Oxford. I am very lucky to be supervised by Prof. Antonis Papachristodoulou and Prof. Kostas Margellos. Before joining Oxford, I did my B.S. in information security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and worked as a research assistant at FINS Lab.

My research interest mainly lies in control theory and optimization, with applications to robotics and smart cities. I am especially interested in Safety, which is a basic property of dynamical systems like Stability.

My Research

  • Decentralized Control

    Decentralized safe control design and safety verification for multi-agent systems.

  • Safety Synthesis

    Safe-in-construction controller design with Sum-of-Sqaures Programming (SOSP).

  • Robotics Testbed

    Digital twins-enabled multi-robot testbed establishment.

  • Motion Planning

    Motion planning for mobile robotics and manipulators.
